Prawn Curry

Description

This simple recipe is quick and requires only a handful of ingredients, most of which you probably have to hand!

Ingredients

  • Enough olive oil to generously coat your pan
  • A large knob of butter
  • One onion, diced
  • 5 or 6 cloves of garlic, diced
  • A thumb-sized piece of ginger, diced
  • 1 thinly chopped green chilli
  • 4 tomatoes, core removed and cut into eighths
  • Half a level teaspoon of salt
  • One heaped teaspoon of turmeric
  • A generous pinch of cumin seeds
  • 3/4 level teaspoon of paprika
  • Half a level teaspoon of crushed black pepper
  • Half a level teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 400 grams of prawns
  • A handful of coriander, roughly chopped (and a generous pinch for garnishing)

Steps

  1. Add the olive oil, butter, onion, garlic and ginger to the pan on a medium heat.
  2. Once they start to turn golden brown, add the chilli and spices, stirring regularly to ensure the mixture does't burn.
  3. After a few minutes, add the prawns and turn the heat up so they cook well. Stir regularly to ensure the mixture is cooked evenly.
  4. After 4 or 5 minutes, add the tomatoes.
  5. Stir regularly every 3 or 4 minutes, using your cooking spoon to pur√©e the the tomatoes.
  6. Cook for 20 - 25 minutes, until enough water has evaporated so the mixture becomes a thick sauce.
  7. Add the chopped coriander and stir through.
  8. Serve into a bowl and garnish with some whole coriander leaves.
